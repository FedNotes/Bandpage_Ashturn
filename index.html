Biography

Ashturn is a metal/hardcore band from Bunsbeek - Belgium founded in 2003.
We see ourselves as "Five guys making metal music".

Ashturn is:
Raf (Vocals)
Mark (Guitar)
Dennis (Guitar)
Tom (Bass)
Steve (Drums)
